About Me

I began my mathematical journey when I was 11, where my team were runners up in the global Maths Quest competition. Soon after, I got hooked into the real-world problem-solving nature of physics too, and went on to develop a deeper understanding of both subjects at the University of Bath. Four years of exams, coursework deadlines, a placement in London, and countless hours in the library went by, concluded by securing a first-class honours.

Despite always having fixed academic interests, I've been through a fair bit of change. I grew up in Malaysia, Norway, and Denmark before coming to the UK for university, even working a summer as a camp counsellor in the US. I love exploring cultures, landscapes, and histories from different corners of the world, and continue to do so with a 6-month trip (starting January 2027) through Oceania & South-East Asia planned and partially booked.

Between now and then, I'll be looking to add some data analysis projects onto my GitHub page, purely for my curiousity. Away from the screen, you may catch me on a run through south-west London or celebrating a goal at the football.
