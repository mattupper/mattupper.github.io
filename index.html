About Me

I really began my mathematical journey when I was 11, where my team finished runners up in the global Maths Quest competition. Naturally I got hooked into the real-world problem-solving nature of physics too, where I ended up focusing my IB Extended Essay on it. I sought to develop a deeper understanding of both subjects, landing me where I am today at the University of Bath.

Despite my academics being constant, my cultural side is anything but. I grew up in Malaysia, Norway, and Denmark before coming to the UK for university, and I've even worked as a camp counsellor in the US during the summer of 2023, which has deeply developed my social skills which help me adapt into new environments with ease.

After I graduate from university in July, I have plans in place to travel for roughly half a year, starting in Australia from January 2027. Between now and then, you may catch me on a run through south-west London or celebrating a goal at the football.
